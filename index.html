About DigiCard

Empowering students with technology

Our aim is to introduce an RFID card to schools which already don't have one. In our school there is a RFID id-card provided to all students. But it only works to provide the attendance of the child with the parents. We plan on expanding the usage of the card. Not only can the card be used for identification purposes, but it can also be used as a wallet. For all these problems to be addressed few RFID readers would be needed in the required locations so that the working of the RFID would become more efficient .
